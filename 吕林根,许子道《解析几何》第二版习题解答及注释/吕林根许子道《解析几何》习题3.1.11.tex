\begin{exercise}
  设从坐标原点到平面$\frac{x}{a}+\frac{y}{b}+\frac{z}{c}=1$的距离为
  $p$,求证
$$
\frac{1}{a^2}+\frac{1}{b^2}+\frac{1}{c^2}=\frac{1}{p^2}.
$$
\end{exercise}
\begin{proof}[\textbf{证明}]
  将平面化为法式方程,为
$$
\frac{\frac{1}{a}}{\sqrt{\frac{1}{a^2}+\frac{1}{b^2}+\frac{1}{c^2}}}x+\frac{\frac{1}{b}}{\sqrt{\frac{1}{a^2}+\frac{1}{b^2}+\frac{1}{c^2}}}y+\frac{\frac{1}{c}}{\sqrt{\frac{1}{a^2}+\frac{1}{b^2}+\frac{1}{c^2}}}z-\frac{1}{\sqrt{\frac{1}{a^2}+\frac{1}{b^2}+\frac{1}{c^2}}}=0.
$$
于是命题成立.
\end{proof}
